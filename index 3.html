Designed for the future

Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the look and feel of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

Powerful tooling for modern content teams

Caching every asset means ultra-fast load times for all your visitors. Blogr's world-class CDN ensures your content reaches your audience at peak performance wherever they are in the world.

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.

Powerful tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing even the most complicated sites.
